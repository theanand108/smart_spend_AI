# UI Principles

Every UI decision must satisfy these principles.

---

## Principle 1

Every card answers one question.

If it doesn't,

remove it.

---

## Principle 2

Whitespace is a feature.

Do not fill empty space unnecessarily.

---

## Principle 3

Typography creates hierarchy.

Do not rely on colors or borders.

---

## Principle 4

Every component earns its place.

No decorative cards.

No meaningless badges.

No filler text.

---

## Principle 5

Insights over Charts.

Charts explain.

Insights guide.

---

## Principle 6

One Primary Action.

Every screen should have exactly one dominant CTA.

---

## Principle 7

Consistency.

Buttons.

Cards.

Inputs.

Spacing.

Radius.

Animation.

Everything follows one system.

---

## Principle 8

Motion supports understanding.

Never animation for animation's sake.

---

## Principle 9

Accessibility first.

Readable fonts.

Proper contrast.

Keyboard navigation.

Clear focus states.

---

## Principle 10

Every screen should answer one question.

Homepage

"What is Smart Spend AI?"

Dashboard

"How am I doing financially?"

Explore

"Why?"

Simulator

"What happens if I add a transaction?"
